Collard Greens

Southern Greens Recipe

Description

A Southern dish that can be made with several variations of greens an cuts of pork

Spice may be added using crushed red pepper before simmering, or any hot sauce/ peppers the chef may want to mix in.

Ingredients

  • Choice of greens
  • Bacon
  • 1/2 Chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken stock

Steps

  1. Cut greens from stem
  2. Wash leaves in colqnder
  3. Roll 3-4 leaves up and cut into strips
  4. In a large pot, start frying 3-4 strips of bacon over medium low-heat
  5. While bacon is frying, peel and dice the onion, throw it in with the bacon to saute
  6. When the bacon is crispy, crumble it into small pieces over the onion
  7. Add the chopped and washed greens into the pot, stirring occasionally until they have cooked down (2-3 minutes)
  8. Pour chicken stock into pot, bring to a boild and then reduce to a simmer
  9. Simmer on a low heat for 45 minutes to an hour, serve hot, and enjoy.
