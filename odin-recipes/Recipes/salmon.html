Glazed Salmon

Origional Recipe

Description

This recipe has been a favorite of mine for a long time. I've made it the same way for years, but feel free to pice it up a bit with the recipe shown in the provided link

My version only has three main ingredients besides the salmon, but it still provides a restaurant qulity dish that will easily impress guests. The ingredient amounts will provide enough glaze for about two pieces of 8oz salmon cuts, add or reduce as needed.

Ingredients

  • Two cuts of salmon
  • 1 tblsp of molassass
  • 1 tblsp of brown mustard
  • Freshly squeezed lemon to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Mix molassas, mustard, and lemon in a small bowl
  3. Brush the glaze over both pieces of salmon until they are thoroughly coated
  4. Place salmon in the oven once it reaches temperature for 12-15 minutes
